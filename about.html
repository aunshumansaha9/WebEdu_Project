Here's a glimpse of what we offer at WebEdu



About Us

We at WebEdu aim for bettering the lives of Special Children through Technology. However alarming the situation seems to be, there aren’t many such websites/applications in the market till date. This venture will add ease and facility all in one place for those who need it most.

This website is basically for parents / guardians who have noticed some changes / symptoms in their child, but weren’t sure of what to do, or seek educational help for their Gifted child/children. Also this website would be useful for teachers and faculty who have less knowledge about the education of the special child and want to explore further regarding this topic.
Reading is kept simple and in an orderly manner as one can read through this document consecutively referring the index..




Our Motivation

Most initiatives/apps focus on basic teaching of special children. In the increasing light of unawareness, our model aims at suggesting parents if their child requires medical attention or not. This matter should be alarming enough to create a positive, healthy and urgent response so as to decrease such cases from being ignored. We aim to attain sufficient level of functionality in order to suggest need for medical attention, treatment, and/or therapeutic methods according to the age group specified.

Strength

  1. Helps educate those aren’t willing to believe
  2. Increases awareness regardless of the user type.
  3. Simple and Easy to understand

Objective

  1. Aided schools in rural/ underdeveloped areas make use of our website.
  2. We hope that this platform can be used by the government so that we could reach out to larger masses
  3. Teachers can recommend this website to parents of special children who seek help in teaching them
